Our Story

The Pipe Prince Story

Built on a royal standard of service, trust, and technical excellence since 2010.

From Humble Beginnings
to Karachi's Royal Plumbers

Pipe Prince was founded in 2010 with a single van, two skilled plumbers, and a mission to deliver plumbing services that homeowners could genuinely trust. In a market plagued by unreliable contractors and surprise fees, we chose a different path.

Over 15 years, we've grown into Karachi's most sought-after plumbing company — serving thousands of residential and commercial clients across the city with the same unwavering commitment to quality that defined us from day one.

Today, our team of 30+ licensed professionals handles everything from emergency callouts to full-scale commercial plumbing projects, each one delivered with what we call the Royal Standard.
